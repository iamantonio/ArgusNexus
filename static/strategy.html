üìä Donchian Channels (55-Period)

The Donchian Channel plots the highest high and lowest low over the last 55 periods. Breakouts above the upper channel signal LONG entries; breakdowns below signal SHORT entries.

üìà LONG Entry Rules

  • Price breaks above 55-period high
  • Price above 200 SMA (bullish trend)
  • Volume > 1.5x 20-period average
  • All three timeframes align

üìâ SHORT Entry Rules (v6.1)

  • Price breaks below 55-period low
  • Price below 200 SMA (bearish trend)
  • Volume > 1.5x 20-period average
  • Position size: 50% of normal
